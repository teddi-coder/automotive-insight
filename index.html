About the workshop

30 +
years in Shenton Park

We started as a European specialist in Shenton Park and never left. Thirty-plus years later, the same principle holds: when you pick up your car, you talk to the person who worked on it. Not a service advisor. Not someone reading from a screen.

That means you find out what was wrong, what was fixed, and why. In plain English. If something didn't need doing yet, we'll tell you that too.

We're one of the few independents in Perth with a DOT inspection licence, which means we can issue roadworthy certificates directly. No sending you elsewhere for a second appointment.
