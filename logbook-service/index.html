What logbook servicing covers

Everything the manufacturer requires — and nothing you don't.

  • Engine oil and filter replaced to manufacturer specification
  • All fluid levels checked and topped up (brake, coolant, power steering, washer)
  • Air filter, cabin filter, and fuel filter replaced per schedule
  • Tyre condition, pressure, and tread depth inspected
  • Brake pad and rotor condition assessed
  • Logbook signed, dated, and stamped at our workshop
