Our story

One workshop.
Thirty years.
Same address.

Automotive Insight opened its doors on Stubbs Terrace in 1993. The workshop has grown, the tools have changed, and we've added EV and hybrid capability as the fleet on Perth roads has evolved — but the fundamentals haven't shifted an inch. You talk to the mechanic. Not a service advisor reading from a screen.

We built the business on European marques at a time when independent workshops either avoided them or guessed at them. Thirty years of working on Audi, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Volkswagen, and Volvo means we diagnose faster and get it right the first time. The same rigour applies to every car that comes through the door, regardless of make.

When something needs doing, we'll tell you clearly — what it is, why it matters, and whether it's urgent. When it can wait, we'll say that too. No inflated estimates, no bundled work that wasn't asked for. That's how we've kept the same customers coming back for decades, and how we keep earning new ones.

What we stand for

What we stand for

Honest diagnosis
We'll tell you what's wrong before we fix it. No work starts without your approval, and we won't pad the quote with things that aren't needed. If it can wait, we'll say so — and put it in writing.
Talk to the mechanic
When you pick up your car, you speak with the person who worked on it. Not an advisor relaying information. Not a front desk translating a report. The mechanic. Direct, clear, no middleman.
No second tier
European, Japanese, hybrid, EV — every car gets the same level of attention. We don't have a budget service and a premium service. There's one standard, and every vehicle that comes through our door gets it.
