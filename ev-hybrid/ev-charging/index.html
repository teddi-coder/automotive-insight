Our charging setup

Onsite charging — no waiting around.

Your EV plugs in as soon as it arrives at the workshop, so charging runs in the background the entire time we're working through the service items. By the time we're done with the car, the battery is well on its way — or topped up entirely.

  • AC charging available onsite
  • Vehicle charges throughout the service
  • No special booking required — just mention your EV when you call
  • We'll let you know the charge level when you collect

While your EV charges

We're not just watching the meter.

We run full mechanical inspections, tyre rotations, brake checks, and any diagnostics while the vehicle charges — the charging time doesn't go to waste. Every minute on charge is a minute we're working through your car properly.

  • Full mechanical inspection
  • Tyre condition and pressure check
  • Brake system inspection
  • Software and OEM diagnostic check
